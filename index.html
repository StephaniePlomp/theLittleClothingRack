How to Hang

Measure and mark the ceiling where you need to drill the two holes.

Step 2

Step 3

First place the plugs are in the holes, then place the screws in the plugs.

Step 4

About us

The idea of the little Clothing Rack originated when my sister needed some extra space for hanging the baby clothes in the nursery room. The nursery room didn't fit a wardrobe so that's why we came up with the idea of making a hanging clothing rack. Besides the fact that she didn't have to buy an expensive wardrobe, it also looks very nice and she can show the most beautiful outfits and mix it in the interior of the nursery room.
